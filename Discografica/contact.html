Who Are We?

Solomun't

Solomun't is a record company producing since 2002. Starting by producing for small artists and commissions when we had less than 10 workers, today we count with more than 300 workers.

Since then we have been increasing our influence until becoming one of the biggest record companies in the international panorama. Producing for the top artists in the industry such as Logic, rickyedit, Trueno, etc.

We try to work with artists of diverse nationalities (EEUU, Spain, Argentina, etc) because we appreciate the influence of every culture in today's global culture.

We have no plans of stopping here. We plan to expand even more our influence in musical culture by contracting more artists, big and small, to provide a medium for them to express their art and reach millions of people.
